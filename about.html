A Rich History

Why Ceylon Sapphires
Are Coveted Worldwide

Sri Lanka (formerly known as Ceylon) boasts some of the most saturated and structurally perfect gemstone veins on earth. Unlike other regions where stones require chemical treatments or extreme heat diffusion to display color, Ceylon Sapphires are renowned for their natural rich saturation, high clarity, and fiery brilliance in their native unheated states.

Historically, Ceylon Sapphires have adorned the royal crowns and jewelry of monarchs worldwide. From the ancient Roman emperors to the famous royal engagement ring of Princess Diana (featuring a 12-carat Ceylon Blue Sapphire), these gems represent the absolute pinnacle of luxury and investment-grade value.

"Sri Lankan gemstones are defined by their unique crystal shape, light refraction properties, and historical value. Sourcing unheated Ceylon stones is the ultimate goal of gem collectors."

Unheated Quality

About Finger Gems & Jewelry

Finger Gems & Jewelry was established with a singular mission: to bring the breathtaking natural beauty of Sri Lankan gemstones directly to international clients. Sourced directly from our family networks and licensed mines in Ratnapura (the historic "City of Gems"), we curate loose collector-grade gemstones and manufacture bespoke fine jewelry.

By connecting online directly with buyers, we bypass intermediate wholesale layers. This enables us to provide absolute transparency, direct mine-to-hand pricing, and fully custom jewelry customization. We stand behind the authenticity of every single crystal we represent.
