Bespoke Process

How We Create Bespoke

01

Stone Selection

First, select your main gemstone. We consult on carat weight, cut types, and blue sapphire hues to fit your budget.

02

CAD Design & Render

Our jewelry designers create a photorealistic 3D CAD render of the design. You review details and approve the design.

03

Handcrafting

Sri Lankan legacy artisans cast the metal (18K Gold, Platinum) and manually hand-set every accent diamond and gemstone.

04

Certification & Delivery

The finished piece undergoes rigorous polishing, obtains formal gemological certificates, and is safely shipped with fully insured global couriers.
